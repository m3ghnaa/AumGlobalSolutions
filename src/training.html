Training and Development

At Aum Global Solutions, we are committed to providing high-quality training and development programs that meet the specific needs of organizations and their employees. Whether you are looking to develop soft skills, technical skills, leadership skills, or sales and marketing skills, we have the expertise and resources to help you achieve your goals.

Communication Skills Training Module:

Effective communication is essential in any business, and our training module on communication skills can help employees to improve their ability to communicate effectively in both verbal and written formats. In this module, we will cover the following topics:

  • Verbal Communication
  • Written Communication
  • Presentation Skills
  • Meeting Skills

Leadership Development Training Module:

Our leadership development module is designed to help employees at all levels to develop their leadership skills. In this module, we will cover the following topics:

  • Leadership Styles
  • Team Building
  • Communication Skills
  • Decision Making

Time Management Training Module:

Time management is critical in today's fast-paced business environment, and our training module can help employees to manage their time more effectively. In this module, we will cover the following topics:

  • Prioritization
  • Goal Setting
  • CTechnology Tools

Project Management Training Module:

Our project management module can help employees to develop their project management skills, including planning, scheduling, budgeting, and risk management. In this module, we will cover the following topics:

  • Project Planning
  • Project Execution
  • Budgeting
  • Risk Management

Emotional Intelligence:

Emotional intelligence refers to the ability to recognize and manage emotions in oneself and others. Our training module on emotional intelligence can help employees to develop skills that are essential for effective communication, collaboration, and leadership. This module includes training on the following topics:

  • Developing self-awareness
  • Building empathy
  • Regulating emotions

Cross-Cultural Communication:

In today's globalized business environment, it is essential for employees to be able to communicate effectively with people from different cultures. Our cross-cultural communication training module can help employees to develop skills that are essential for building strong relationships with customers, colleagues, and partners from around the world. This module includes training on the following topics:

  • Building cultural awareness
  • Adapting communication styles
  • Developing intercultural competence

Innovation and Creativity:

Innovation and creativity are essential for organizations that want to stay competitive and meet the changing needs of their customers. Our training module on innovation and creativity can help employees to develop the skills and mindset that are necessary for generating new ideas and driving innovation. This module includes training on the following topics:

  • Brainstorming effectively
  • Using design thinking methodologies
  • Creating a culture of innovation
