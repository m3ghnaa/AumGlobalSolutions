Contract Labour Supply

At Aum Global Solutions, we understand the importance of contract labour in any organization. Contract labour can help companies to manage their workforce requirements in a flexible and cost- effective manner. We specialize in providing high-quality contract labour supply services to meet the varying needs of our clients across different industries.

Our contract labour supply services cover a wide range of job profiles including skilled, semi-skilled and unskilled labour. We provide workers on a contractual basis to meet the specific project requirements of our clients. We ensure that our workers are well-trained, experienced and capable of working in different environments and conditions.

Our contract labour supply services also include compliance with all statutory regulations related to contract labour. We take care of all legal and administrative aspects of contract labour such as registration, licensing, payment of minimum wages, and compliance with labour laws.

We understand that payroll processing is a critical aspect of your organization's operations, and that is why we offer all-encompassing services that guarantee the industry's best and perfect payroll solutions. We offer three layers of services to match a range of organizational requirements, including payroll processing and compliance, employee self-service, and query resolution.

Our team of experts works closely with clients to understand their specific labour requirements and provide customized solutions. We also provide on-site supervision and support to ensure that our workers perform to the best of their abilities and deliver high-quality results.

By partnering with Aum Global Solutions for contract labour supply services, our clients can focus on their core business activities while leaving the workforce management to us. We ensure that our clients receive the best-in-class contract labour supply services that are flexible, reliable and cost- effective.
